About Us


SKYLE is located in Pyin Oo Lwin, hill sides near An. SKYLE Hotel was officially opened in 15th, July, 2019. It is build primarily to be private and reduce stress. it's with the best services for you, beautiful mount views, delicious traditional food, best cleaning services and acceptable prices for your rooms. Come and visit to our SKYLE hotel and taste the real enjoyment of your life.
